All About Little Mix

Description

Little Mix are a British girl group. The group consists of Perrie Edwards, Jade Thirlwall, and Leigh-Anne Pinnock. One of it's original members, Jesy Nelson, departed from the group in 2020. The group music style is of pop, R&B and dance-pop, with influences from other genres such as tropical house, Latin pop and electronic music, with lyrics that are inspired by female empowerment, body positivity, LGBT rights and the group's own experiences with sexism.
